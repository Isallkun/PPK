Desa Wonokitri.

Desa Wonokitri is a village located in the Tosari district of Pasuruan Regency, East Java, Indonesia. It is situated at an elevation of 2,215 meters above sea level. Desa Wonokitri is known as a tourist village that supports the Bromo Tengger Semeru National Park. It is one of four villages that are recognized as tourist villages in the area. Visitors can reach Desa Wonokitri by using public transportation such as Bus Damri, travel or rental cars from Pasuruan City to Warungdowo - Ranggeh - Pasrepan - Puspo - Tosari - Wonokitri - Bromo. However, private vehicles are not allowed to enter Bromo and can only go up to the rest area. There are several attractions in Desa Wonokitri such as traditional houses and Edelweiss flowers. The village is still rich in customs and traditions that have been passed down from generation to generation. Visitors can also enjoy the cool air with temperatures ranging from 12°C at dawn to around 20°C during the day.
